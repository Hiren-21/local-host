Banglore

Bangalore is the capital of the southern state of Karnataka and is Asia's fastest growing cosmopolitan city. The city's role as the nation's leading Information Technology exporter has earned it the sobriquet, 'Silicon Valley of India'.Located at a height of 949 m above sea level, this capital city of Karnataka has something to offer for everyone.

Kerala

Kerala, located on the south-western tip of India, enjoys unique geographical features that have made it one of the most sought-after tourist destinations in Asia. Kerala was selected by the National Geographic Traveller as one of the 50 destinations of a lifetime and one of the thirteen paradises in the world.
